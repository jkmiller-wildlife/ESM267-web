Map of Western Snowy Plover nests at Vandenberg Air Force Base, 2011-2018, separated by hatch or failure. North and South Base nests may be examined by zooming in on specific areas.

Mouse-over and click on a nest for the following attributes: N_UTM_WGS84; E_UTM_WGS84; Year; Nest_ID; Block_Name; Restoration_Area; Date_Found; Eggs_When_Found; Initiation_Date; Fate_Date; Fate_Category; Fate, Num_Eggs; Num_Chicks; Num_Fledged; Notes (if applicable); Proofed_Initials
